# Noclip AT

Noclip AT lets you choose a percent where noclip activates.

For example, if you set it to **50%**, you play normally from **0% to 49%**. Once you reach **50%**, noclip turns on and deaths are ignored for the rest of the attempt.

## Default menu keybind

The default keybind to open the Noclip AT menu is **F8**.

### How to change the keybind

1. Open **Geode**
2. Go to **Installed**
3. Select **Noclip AT**
4. Click the **gear/settings** button
5. Change **Open Noclip AT Menu** to any key you want

## How to use

1. Open the Noclip AT menu with **F8**
2. Type the percent where noclip should start
3. Click **ON/OFF** to enable or disable the mod
4. Start a level and play normally until the chosen percent
5. Once you reach that percent, noclip turns on automatically

## Intended use

Noclip AT is meant for practice, showcases, testing routes, and learning level endings.

This mod should not be used to claim legitimate completions.
